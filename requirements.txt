Transformers  
A library from Hugging Face that provides pre-trained AI models for tasks like text summarization, translation, question answering, and more.
In this project, it’s used to load a summarization model  that turns raw logs into human-readable summaries.

Torch  
Short for PyTorch, this is the deep learning framework that powers most Hugging Face models. It handles tensor operations, training, and running neural networks efficiently.
 Think of it as the “engine” that runs the AI models behind the scenes.
